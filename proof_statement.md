## Part 3: AI Interview Transcript (Narrowing the Claim)
Below is the discussion run with my AI partner to narrow down my claim, audience, and action.

*   **AI Critique:** *"Recruiters look at keywords, but the person who actually validates if your model is good and makes the final hire is the Lead ML Engineer. Speak directly to them."*
*   **Refinement:** Target the **Lead Machine Learning Engineer / Search Data Scientist**.

### 3. Choosing the "One Action"
*   **Initial Draft:** *"Get hired as an intern."*
*   **AI Critique:** *"You can't get hired directly from a landing page. What is the realistic next step in the pipeline?"*
*   **Refinement:** Get them to **invite me to a technical interview to walk through my repository code**.
